Tom Holland is an English actor, best known for playing Spider Man in the Marvel Cinematic Universe. He started his career in theater, appearing in Billy Elliot the Musical. Holland gained fame in 2016 with his role in Captain America: Civil War and has since starred in Spider-Man: Homecoming and its sequels. He has also appeared in films like The Impossible and Chaos Walking. Holland is praised for his acting skills and is one of Hollywood's top young stars.
